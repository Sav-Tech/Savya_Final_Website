ABOUT ME

Hello Everyone! My name is Savya Sarda and this is my personal website. I am 13 years old and currently in Grade 7 (Year 8). My hobbies are traveling, playing sports, reading books, writing essays, and watching TV. My favorite sports are badminton, soccer, basketball, and running. My favorite reading genre is action and adventure.

I have lived in three different countries during my life. I was born in India, New Dehli and after 4 years of living there, I moved to the Philipines. After living for 3 years in the Philippines, I moved to Hong Kong, where I am currently living. Hence, I can speak three languages: English, Hindhi, and Mandarin. I have also traveled in Singapore, China, and Japan!

I have a family of four people. My mom, my dad, and my twin brother. My mom is a Grade 1 teacher at American International School Hong Kong (AIS). My dad works for one of the largest insurance companys in the world called AIA! Me and my brother study in AIS.
